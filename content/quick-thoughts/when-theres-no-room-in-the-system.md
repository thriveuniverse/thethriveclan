---
title: When you need to ask for more, but there's no room in the system
---

Costs are rising, salaries are stagnant, and now another cost lands. Or morale's low, workload's high, and a new crisis stacks on top. Can you get a positive out of only negatives? Honestly — maybe not. But a reduced negative is often enough. The goal was never to fix the whole environment with one decision; it's to move forward, because every step forward builds its own small buffer against where you were. That's why the environment matters as much as the crisis sitting inside it. Good management isn't solving everything. It's balancing what you can control against what you can't, one workable step at a time.
